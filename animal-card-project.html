Bottlenose dolphin

Bottlenose dolphins sharing close ratios of encephalization levels with humans and other great apes, which more than likely contributes to their high intelligence and emotional intelligence.

  • Scientific name: Tursiops
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Length: 6.6 - 13 feet
  • Gestation period: 15 - 18 months

With a cute "smiling face" and extremely high intelligence, bottlenose dolphins have gained popularity from aquarium shows and television programs such as Flipper.
